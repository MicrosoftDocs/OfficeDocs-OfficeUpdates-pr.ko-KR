> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="b04d6-106">최신 버전의 Office 2013은 2020년 3월 13일에 릴리스된 **15.0.5223.1001**입니다.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b04d6-106">The most current version of Office 2013 is **15.0.5223.1001**, which was released on March 13, 2020.</span></span>
