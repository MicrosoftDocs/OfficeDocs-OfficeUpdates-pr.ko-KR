> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="aa4f7-106">최신 버전의 Office 2013은 2021년 6월 8일에 릴리스된 **15.0.5357.1000** 입니다.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aa4f7-106">The most current version of Office 2013 is **15.0.5357.1000**, which was released on June 8, 2021.</span></span>
